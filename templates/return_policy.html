Return Policy


Returns & Refunds for xtayconnectafrica.com


What products can I return?

All products sold on xtayconnectafrica.com can be returned for free provided certain conditions are met such as: Item state, Full attachments and accessories availability, First level investigation results match your return reason Etc. However, note that we have several restrictions on some product categories due to their sensitivity.

What are the conditions for my return?

For your purchase to be eligible for return, you shall be able to return it in 5 days after delivery provided that:

Condition A:

If the product is not as you expected/not as advertised. If you don’t want the product anymore. Then the product must be returned unused, in its original packaging and with all seals & tags intact.

Condition B:

If the product is damaged on arrival, defective on arrival or counterfeit. If your product is the wrong size. Then the product must be returned in its original packaging. All accessories, tags, and free gifts must also be returned.

How can I return my product?
Step 1: Log your return request 1.Contact our customer support agents

2.A “Request a return form” will be provided to you. Complete the full return form by giving exact and detailed information. Caution: Providing wrong information might result in your return rejection or refund to a wrong recipient, and xtayconnectafrica.com’s shall not be responsible.

Step 2: Pack your product

Pack your product according to the return conditions. Include all tags, accessories or free gifts you received


Step 3: Return your product

1.If you selected a drop-off, please drop your package at the chosen place following the given instructions. Note that the sooner you drop your item, the sooner your refund will be processed.

2.If you selected a return pickup (provided you were eligible for that return option), we will contact you to arrange a pickup time.

How long do I have to initiate my return?
Dependent on your eligibility to return product, you will be able to return it in 5 days after delivery. In other terms, if you received your order on the 5th, then you have until the 10th to request a return on any of the items received.

In what condition am I refunded?
A refund can be expected in any of the below situations: You canceled a prepaid order
You rejected a prepaid order
You returned an eligible item within 5 days as per xtayconnectafrica.com’s return policy

How will I get my refund?
Basically, refunds are done using the same payment method used when placing the order (in case of prepaid orders). However, in the event that you request for a return, the refund method shall be as per the refund method you selected while making your purchase.

How long would it take to get my refund?
Once your item has been retrieved, it shouldn’t take more than 10 days to receive your refund, provided the return matches our returns conditions. Also, to prevent refund delay:

1.Create your return request soon enough with full necessary information (Return reasons, phone number, refund method, etc.)

2.Drop your item as soon as possible

Do I have to pay for shipping charges when I return a product?
Returning an item is free! As soon as you’ve returned the item, xtayconnectafrica.com will take care of the rest.

Do I have to return all the products in my order?
Of course, not! You do not have to return your whole order; you can return certain product(s), but with their respective accessories or labels, though.

What if my returned item is not cleared for refund?

If your return is not cleared for a refund, then we will call you to explain the issue and send the item(s) back to you. We will arrange the delivery or drop-off of the item.

Please note that after 3 attempts to return the product to you, we will cancel the return procedure and will keep the product with us for a maximum of 60 days.

Can I return my items after the timeline to return has expired?
Sorry, returns are not possible after xtayconnectafrica.com’s 5 days return period. However, if you experienced some issues after xtayconnectafrica.com’s return period has elapsed, you can claim your warranty (if applicable) from the brand’s authorized service center.

I haven’t received my refund amount in my bank account yet. What do I do?
Please rest assured that your account will be credited within 7 to 10 working days after the acceptance of your return. If this does not happen, do not hesitate to contact us.

I have requested for my product to be returned. What happens now?

1.Provided that you are within Accra and Tema and you specifically chose the “pickup” return option, we’ll schedule for it to be picked up from your address.
Requests received before 10 am might be picked up on the same day or the next business day. Requests received after 2 pm will be picked up on the next two business days.

2.If you are outside Accra and Tema, you will have to drop-off the item at one of the service centers of our logistics partner. The service center shall be the one you selected while submitting your purchase request from your Account.

Once your item has been retrieved, expect a duration of 10 days to receive your refund, if the return meets our conditions for return.

Do I have to return the free gift when I return a product?
Yes, any free gift must also be returned as it’s likely to be part of the package that will have to be returned to its supplier.

What happens when my returned product is received back at xtayconnectafrica.com’s Warehouse?
Once your product is received, we carry out the necessary checks to ensure that the returned item fulfills the required conditions. Hence, expect an overall duration of 10 days to receive your refund if the return meets our conditions for return.
